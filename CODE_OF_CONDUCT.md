# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Civil Toolbox is committed to providing a welcoming environment for all contributors, regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

We value technical excellence, constructive collaboration, and respectful communication.

---

## Expected Behavior

### Technical Collaboration

- Engage in constructive technical discussion
- Support claims with evidence, references, or reasoning
- Acknowledge when you don't know something
- Give credit where due
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully

### Communication

- Be respectful and professional
- Assume good intent
- Focus on the work, not the person
- Keep discussions on-topic
- Be patient with contributors at all experience levels

### Engineering Integrity

- Prioritize correctness over convenience
- Document assumptions and limitations honestly
- Report calculation errors promptly
- Cite sources for engineering methods

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Personal attacks or harassment
- Dismissive or demeaning comments
- Deliberately misleading technical claims
- Publishing others' private information
- Sustained disruption of discussions
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

---

- Links or screenshots if applicable
- Any context that may be relevant

---

## Enforcement

Maintainers may take action including:

- Warning the individual
- Temporary or permanent ban from the project
- Removal of contributions that violate this policy

Enforcement decisions are at maintainer discretion and are final.

---

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to:

- Repository discussions (issues, PRs, comments)
- Project communication channels
- Events or meetings related to the project
- Any context where someone represents the project
